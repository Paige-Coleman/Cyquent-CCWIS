How Save & Restore Works

No account needed — your work is saved in this browser

1
Open either demo above. A CYQUENT save toolbar appears in the bottom-right corner of the screen.
2
Work through the demo — fill forms, navigate cases, log contacts. Your work auto-saves every 30 seconds and on every form change.
3
Close the browser tab. Reopen the same demo URL. Click ↩ Restore — or it auto-restores your last save.
4
To hand off your session, click ⬇ Export to download a JSON file. Send it to a colleague, who clicks ⬆ Import to load your exact state.
5
Your Session ID (shown above) is unique to this browser. If you need support, share it. Click the ID chip in the toolbar to copy it instantly.
6
Important: Data is stored only in this browser (localStorage). Clearing browser data or switching devices starts fresh — use Export/Import to move sessions.
